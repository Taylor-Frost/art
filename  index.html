About Me

My name is Taylor Frost, an 18 year old primarily self-taught digital painter who specializes in colorful expressive art. I have been painting for 7 years now, fitting it in between my other interests. I always feel like an artist should have as many styles as possible to market to larger audiences, so my quest has been to learn as much as I can. I pride myself in creating detailed, emotional portraits and pieces of both animals and people that are the perfect gift for family or to be hung up on a wall. I adore painting endangered animals to raise awareness of their situation. My dream is to be able to create money through prints and more from my animal paintings to be able to give a portion to efforts that help the very endangered species I paint so that they can be around as a muse for many years. I hope that viewers enjoyed this peak into my life and would like to see more of my work, embarking on this fantastic artistic journey with me!

    More About Me

    I enjoy playing with vibrant colors and glowing light in my pieces, in a way that is not hyper realistic but adds to the emotions and energy of a painting. I like to say that I don't copy reality, but I add to it, which in a way makes it hyper-reality. I live in both Canada and Costa Rica, traveling between the two throughout the year.
